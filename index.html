About

Mac N Cheese was created by a group of seven men who originally, were all on a baseball team together as young kids. These men would always run home to Timothy Higglesworth's home after a long day of practice and eat Mrs Higglesworth's famous mac n cheese. The boys were hooked on it and one day in 1957 they decided to take matters into their own hands and create their own mac n cheese restaurant where the possibilities of mac n cheese were endless.

Menu

Our Menu consists of a diverse group of Mac N Cheese options ranging from our classic Mac N Cheese to our Mac N Cheese Waffle. No matter what you decide on your taste buds will be fulfilled with cheesey goodness and satisfaction.
