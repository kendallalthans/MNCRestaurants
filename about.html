How was MNC founded?

Mac N Cheese was created by a group of seven men who originally, were all on a baseball team together as young kids. These men would always run home to Timothy Higglesworth's (all the way left) home after a long day of practice and eat Mrs Higglesworth's famous mac n cheese. The boys were hooked on it and one day in 1957 they decided to take matters into their own hands and create their own mac n cheese restaurant where the possibilities of mac n cheese were endless. (From left: Timothy Higglesworth, Ronald Simpson, Clark Rine, Joe Stiffins, Jim Boggle, Lenny Stove, Mark Lich)
